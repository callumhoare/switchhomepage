What is the Switch Launchpad?

The switch launchpad is a website created to be ran on the Nintendo Switch system to allow the Switch to connect to the internet though the internal browser in the "Connect to this network" in the Switch's internet settings.

The Switch Launchpad has a modified .farx file baised from the "Fiddler Browse Anywhere Method" from here.


Does this involve any sort of hacking? I don't want to break my new console!!!

No, you do not need to hack your switch.
The only thing you will have to do is set some proxy settings on the switch itself,
but that option is already on the switch right out of the box!
